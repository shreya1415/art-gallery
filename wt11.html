    The Art Cafe presents a curated collection of affordable, contemporary Indian Art by India's finest emerging and established artists

    Welcome to The Art Cafe, the one-stop destination for all the art lovers. Art has always been an integral part of society, not today but since the time humans had started civilizing. Not just the photographic presentation of the shapes around, art also tends to depict and share the complete experience of the moment enveloped in those photographs. Love, passion, and admiration of art stem up the basis of The Art Cafe. Each artist associates himself with a certain school of imagination. With 1000+ artists and over 16,000 artworks on display, The Art Cafe has the presence of multitudinous artworks by various artists. Diversity, multifariousness and variegation best describe the myriad range of artworks The Art Cafe has.
